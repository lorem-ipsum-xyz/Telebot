How to get bot token?


Step 1:

Go to your Telegram app


Step 2:

Go to @BotFather and click 'start'


Step 3:

Type /newbot and follow the steps to create a new bot


Step 4:

BotFather will ask you to provide a name and a username for your bot


Step 5:

Once done, BotFather will send you a message containing your bot token. Copy and save this token securely.
